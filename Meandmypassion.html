Me and My Passion

Hello,it is me.
Judy		 Hello, my name is Judy. I am studying Master of computing in my second semester. I have one year of experience working as team member in a non-profit environment organization in US. By finishing my study in US, I decided to achieve different area knowledge in Australia. My past-experience has helped me broaden my vision towards creative and strategic thinking. In my spare time, I am passionate about baking, which is the best way to relax when I have overwhelming pressure on study.
Passion In my free time, I have three passions that bring me immense joy. First and foremost, baking is my biggest love. I relish the process of taking ingredients and turning them into delectable treats, and I thoroughly enjoy troubleshooting any technical challenges that arise during the baking process. Secondly, I have a passion for capturing and expressing my thoughts through both video and written content, which I enjoy sharing on social media. Lastly, my favorite physical activity is playing tennis, and I was even a key member of my university's tennis club. These three passions bring out the best in me and allow me to express myself creatively while also staying active and engaged.
Mission Statement Judy’s Baking World.com is an online baking center sharing recipe, videos, tips and energy to all people who has the same passion on baking. The biggest highlight of this website is all information share here are fully practiced by Judy. We are keen to build a website that can help new learner as Judy at the very beginning. What the website shows is not only the successful results, but also the so-called failure case. Judy’s Baking World will share how to make cakes just in your own kitchen without buying tons of tools in advance. The goal of this website is helping to have fun in baking rather chase a perfect result.
